SwiftDeliver Logistics - Customer Support & Delivery Policy

1. Delivery Timeline
Standard delivery time is 3 to 5 business days depending on location within Pakistan.
Remote areas may take up to 7 business days.

2. Order Confirmation Policy
All orders must be confirmed by the customer before dispatch.
Unconfirmed orders may be placed on hold or cancelled automatically.

3. Address Change Policy
Customers can update their delivery address only before the order is dispatched.
Once the order is shipped, address changes are not allowed.

4. Cancellation Policy
Orders can be cancelled before dispatch without any charges.
Once shipped, cancellation is not possible.

5. Return & Refund Policy
Customers can request a return within 7 days of delivery if the product is damaged or incorrect.
Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days after verification.

6. Cash on Delivery (COD)
COD is available for selected cities only including Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad.
Orders above Rs. 20,000 may require advance confirmation call.

7. Fraud Prevention Policy
Orders with incomplete or suspicious information may be flagged for manual review.
Repeated fake orders may result in blocked service access.

8. Customer Support
Support is available from 9 AM to 9 PM via automated AI assistant.
Urgent issues are escalated to human agents.
